The Apple Puzzle

A guide to what an Apple Puzzle is and how to solve it

Introduction

An Apple Puzzle is a type of Rubix Cube. Instead of different colored parts, the Apple Puzzle is made up of parts varying in shape. Since each part is unique in shape, you're able to keep track of what you've already solved. When the Apple Puzzle is solved, all of the parts are aligned to make the puzzle look like a beautiful apple.
